💡 How It Helps

Enter your weight and height to calculate your Body Mass Index (BMI). This helps you understand whether you are underweight, at a healthy weight, overweight, or obese.

Based on your BMI, you can take the right steps toward your fitness goals — whether it's gaining muscle, losing fat, or maintaining a balanced lifestyle.

For best results, combine regular workouts, a balanced diet, proper hydration, and consistent sleep. Small daily improvements lead to long-term success.
